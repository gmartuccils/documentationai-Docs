## Book Your First Lawn Mow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to `https://www.lawnstarter.com` and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your property">
    Enter your home address and lawn details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select mowing service">
    Choose the mowing package you want (standard, premium, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and pay">
    Review your order and complete payment to schedule your mow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
