## Overview

Scheduling a lawn service with Lawnstarter is simple and quick.

## Steps

1. Log in at `https://app.lawnstarter.com`
2. Click "Book a Service"
3. Choose your address and lawn size
4. Select preferred dates
5. Confirm booking

## Need help?
Contact support if you need assistance with your booking.
